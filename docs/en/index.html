Desktop voice input, kept calm

Speak once. Your words land where you are already working.

AriaType is a local-first desktop app for voice typing. Hold a hotkey, speak naturally, and text appears at the cursor without copy and paste.

How it works

Three steps, kept close to the work

01

Hold your hotkey

Start from the keyboard, not from a dashboard. Trigger voice input from the app you are already using.

02

Speak naturally

Talk at a normal pace while the product keeps recording state and model choice explicit.

03

Release to insert

When the capture ends, the text is typed back where your cursor already is.
