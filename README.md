### What is AriaType?

**AriaType is your private, local voice keyboard.**

It runs quietly in the background. When you want to type, just hold a shortcut key (`Shift+Space` by default), speak naturally, and release. AriaType instantly transcribes your speech and types it into the active application—whether it's VS Code, Slack, Notion, or your browser.

**Local-first. Privacy-first. No data leaves your device.**

### Why use AriaType?

Built for daily writing and messaging—local-first, privacy-first, and effortless:

- **🔒 Local First & Private First**: Your voice data never leaves your computer.
- **✨ AI Polish**: Automatically removes filler words ("um", "uh"), fixes grammar, and formats your text perfectly.
- **🚀 Blazing Fast**: Speak and see text instantly.
- **🌍 100+ Languages**: Supports English, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Japanese, Korean, and more.
- **💻 Cross-Platform**: Native support for macOS (Apple Silicon/Intel) and Windows.

### How it works

1. **Hold** your hotkey (`Shift+Space`).
2. **Speak** your mind.
3. **Release** to see your text appear instantly.

### Getting Started

1. **Install & Launch** AriaType.
2. **Grant Permissions** (Microphone & Accessibility).
3. **Download a Model** (one-time): We recommend the **Base** model for a good balance of speed and accuracy.
4. **Set Your Language**: Auto-detect works great, or lock it to your primary language.
5. **Try it out**: Open a text editor, hold `Shift+Space`, and say "Hello world".
